Tribute To Ada Ameh

The legendary female actor

Biography

Ada Ameh which is born 15 May 1974 and died 17 July 2022, she was a Nigerian actress who spent more than two decades in the Nigerian movie industry and was notable for her character as Anita in the 1996 movie titled Domitilla and as Emu Johnson in the award-winning Nigerian television series titled The Johnsons. Ameh in The Johnsons television series featured alongside other Nollywood actors such as Charles Inojie, Chinedu Ikedieze, and Olumide Oworu.

Achievements

  • Ameh was a veteran Nollywood actress who worked in the Nigerian film industry for over 20 years.
  • Ameh is known for her roles as Anita in the 1996 movie Domitilla and Emu Johnson in the award-winning TV series The Johnsons.
  • Ameh was the CEO of Adylad Productions
  • Ameh was a philanthropist
  • Ameh was a motivational speaker.
